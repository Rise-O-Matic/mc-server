If You Can't Connect

  • "Couldn't connect to the world" — most likely you're not on the allowlist yet. Message Steven your exact gamertag.
  • "Server outdated / client outdated" — update Minecraft on your device.
  • Address rejected — make sure you typed winter-stick.gl.at.ply.gg exactly (no spaces, no http://) and the port is 55838.
  • Server not responding — Steven may have it turned off. Try later or ping him.
